Gravity

This is a simulation of gravity using Newton's law of motion: \(\sum{\overrightarrow{F}} = ma\)
and Newton's law of gravity: \(\overrightarrow{F_{a \rightarrow b}} = G \frac{m_a m_b}{d_{ab}^2} \overrightarrow{u_{ba}}\)
To make things interesting, I've set the particles to repulse themselves if they come too close together. You can play with that parameter using the slider below.

Number of particles: 500
Min distance between particles before repulsion: 50
